INDonesian Resource GrAmmar

INDRA

  • an open-source computational grammar for Indonesian.
  • modelled in Head-Driven Phrase Structure Grammar (HPSG)
    and Minimal Recursion Semantics (MRS).
  • created and developed using tools from DELPH-IN.
  • aims to parse Indonesian text in NTU-MC.
  • will be applied to machine translation.
  • stored in GitHub.

Theoretical framework and tools

Phrase Structure Grammar

  • a set of rules:
    S      → NP VP
    NP → N
    VP → V NP
  • a lexicon of POS and words:
    N : anjing "dog", kucing "cat"
    V : mengejar "chase"
     

What sentences can be generated
from the rules and lexicon above?

  1. Anjing mengejar kucing "Dogs chase cats"
  2. Kucing mengejar anjing "Cats chase dogs"

Type Description Language (TDL)


            anjing := nonhuman-noun-noun-lex & 
              [ STEM < "anjing" >,
                SYNSEM.LKEYS.KEYREL.PRED "_anjing_n_rel" ].

            kejar := tr-verb-lex &
              [ STEM < "kejar" >,
                SYNSEM.LKEYS.KEYREL.PRED "_kejar_v_rel" ].

            kucing := nonhuman-noun-noun-lex &
              [ STEM < "kucing" >,
                SYNSEM.LKEYS.KEYREL.PRED "_kucing_n_rel" ].

Full Forest Treebanker (FFTB)

  • Makes a syntactically annotated corpus by annotating each sentence with a parse tree

Treebanking of anjingnya sedang menggonggong
"the dog is barking"

(Packard, 2014; Jurafsky & Martin, 2009)

Nanyang Technological University Multilingual Corpus (NTU-MC)

  • Parallel corpus, sense-tagged using a lexical database Wordnet
  • Indonesian text data contains 2,197 sentences from Singapore Tourism Board (STB) website
  • ongoing process of adding Sherlock Holmes short stories
  • INDRA aims to parse at least 60% of the Indonesian text

(Tan & Bond, 2012; Bond et al., 2013)

Wordnet Bahasa

  • created based on Princeton Wordnet (PWN), a large English lexical database.
  • groups nouns, verbs, adjectives and adverbs into sets of concepts or synsets.
  • Three synsets of anjing "dog"
    Synset Definition
    02083346-n any of various fissiped mammals...
    10539715-n a person who is deemed to be despicable
    02084071-n a member of the genus Canis...

(Nurril Hirfana et al., 2011; Bond et al., 2014)
