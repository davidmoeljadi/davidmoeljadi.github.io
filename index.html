David MOELJADI

Indonesian, linguist, lecturer, traveler, language lover, ink painting certified instructor

Areas of interest: Indonesian grammar, Old Javanese grammar, wordnets and lexicography, corpus development, Chinese loanwords

Research and projects

Computational linguistics, corpus linguistics

  • Indonesian Resource Grammar (INDRA): a computational grammar for Indonesian
  • JATI: a treebank for formal Indonesian
  • Cendana: a treebank for informal Indonesian
  • Barasa: Indonesian SentiWordNet for sentiment analysis
  • Indonesian Frog Storytelling Corpus: Indonesian written and spoken corpus
  • Old Javanese computational grammar
  • IndoNLP
  • Reduplications in Contemporary Indonesian Grammar (Tata Bahasa Indonesia Kontemporer)
  • Aplikasi Penyuntingan Ejaan Bahasa Indonesia (Sipebi) [Indonesian Spelling Checker Application]
  • Building an Error-Annotated Indonesian Learner Corpus
  • Building a Corpus of Linguistic Acceptability for Malay/Indonesian

Dictionaries and Lexicography

  • Wordnet Bahasa: semantic dictionary of Malay languages (Malaysian and Indonesian)
  • Old Javanese Wordnet: semantic dictionary of Old Javanese
  • Kamus Besar Bahasa Indonesia (KBBI) Daring: online Indonesian dictionary database
  • KBBI Android application, KBBI iOS application: mobile applications for KBBI Indonesian Dictionary
  • Chinese loanwords in Indonesian (Sinophone), Indonesian loanword database
  • Kamus Pemelajar Kanji Jepang–Indonesia (Japanese Kanji–Indonesian Learner's Dictionary): editor-in-chief
  • Onomatopoeia - what's in a name?

Projects I have participated in

  • DFG-Projekt: Information distribution and language structure - correlation of grammatical expressions of the noun/verb distinction and lexical information content in Tagalog, Indonesian and German
  • Modality and Corpora: Analyzing modal strength in Indonesian (a corpus-based study)
  • Verb class analysis accelerator for low-resource languages (The Czech Science Foundation/GAČR project)
  • MALINDO Conc: online concordance system for Malay/Indonesian
  • MALINDO Morph: a morphological dictionary for Malay/Indonesian
  • TALPCo: TUFS Asian Language Parallel Corpus
  • Nanyang Technological University - Multilingual Corpus (NTU-MC)
